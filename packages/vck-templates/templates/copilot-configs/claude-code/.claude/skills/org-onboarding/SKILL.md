# Organization Onboarding

## Purpose

Capture essential organization-wide context that applies across all projects. This information helps Kahuna make better recommendations aligned with your company's industry, constraints, and priorities.

## When to Use

Trigger this skill when:
- User says "set up org context" or "organization onboarding"
- User says "update org context" (re-run to update)
- Project onboarding detects no org context exists (automatic)

## Process

### Step 1: Ask About Industry

Ask the user:

> **What industry or domain do you work in?**
> (e.g., healthcare, fintech, e-commerce, enterprise software, etc.)

Wait for their response.

### Step 2: Ask About Team Structure

Ask the user:

> **Who's involved in building projects here? Solo, small team, or larger organization?**

Wait for their response.

### Step 3: Ask About Constraints

Ask the user:

> **Are there any technical or business constraints we should know about?**
> (e.g., specific platforms, regulatory requirements, budget limits, etc.)

If they say "no" or "not really", that's fine - just note "No specific constraints mentioned."

### Step 4: Ask About Priorities

Ask the user:

> **What matters most to your organization? Speed to market, reliability, compliance, cost efficiency, or something else?**

Wait for their response.

### Step 5: Confirm Summary

Before storing, show the user a summary of what was captured:

> **Here's what I captured:**
> - **Industry:** [their answer]
> - **Team:** [their answer]
> - **Constraints:** [their answer, or "None mentioned"]
> - **Priority:** [their answer]
>
> Does this look right? (say "yes" to continue, or correct anything)

If they say "yes", "looks good", "correct", etc. → proceed to Step 6.
If they provide corrections → update the relevant field and confirm again.

### Step 6: Synthesize and Store

**Step 6a:** Create a file at `/tmp/org-context.md` with this content:

```markdown
# Organization Context

**Created:** [current date]

## Industry & Domain

[Synthesize answer to Question 1 - be concise, 2-3 sentences max]

## Team Structure

[Synthesize answer to Question 2 - be concise]

## Constraints

[Synthesize answer to Question 3, or "No specific constraints mentioned"]

## Priorities

[Synthesize answer to Question 4]
- Primary priority: [main one mentioned]
- Trade-off guidance: [infer from their answer how to make decisions]

---

*Generated by Kahuna organization onboarding | Update with "update org context"*
```

**Step 6b:** Call `kahuna_learn` to store the document in the knowledge base:

```
kahuna_learn(
  paths=["/tmp/org-context.md"],
  description="Organization-wide context: industry, team, constraints, priorities"
)
```

### Step 7: Confirm and Continue

Tell the user:

> I've captured your organization context. This will help me make recommendations aligned with your industry and priorities.
>
> Now let's set up context for this specific project...

Then **automatically proceed to project onboarding** by asking the first project question (see project-onboarding skill).

## Notes

- **One-time setup** - Org context is captured once and reused across projects
- **Quick questions** - Users should complete this in under 3 minutes
- **Updateable** - "update org context" reruns this flow
- **No probing** - Accept brief answers; don't push for more detail
