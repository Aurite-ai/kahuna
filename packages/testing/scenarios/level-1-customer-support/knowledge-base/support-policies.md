# TechFlow Pro - Support Policies

## Support Channels

### Community Forum
- **Available to**: All plans
- **Access**: community.techflow.com
- **Response**: Community-driven; TechFlow staff monitor but don't guarantee responses
- **Best for**: General questions, tips, feature discussions

### Email Support
- **Available to**: Starter, Pro, Enterprise
- **Address**: support@techflow.com
- **Response times**:
  - Starter: 24-48 business hours
  - Pro: 4 business hours
  - Enterprise: 1 business hour
- **Best for**: Account issues, technical problems, billing questions

### Live Chat
- **Available to**: Pro, Enterprise
- **Access**: Click the chat icon in the bottom-right of the app
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 6 AM - 6 PM Pacific Time
- **Response**: Typically under 5 minutes during business hours
- **Best for**: Quick questions, urgent issues during business hours

### Phone Support
- **Available to**: Enterprise only
- **Number**: Provided to Enterprise customers upon signup
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 6 AM - 6 PM Pacific Time
- **Best for**: Complex issues, urgent matters, account management

### Dedicated Success Manager
- **Available to**: Enterprise only
- **Services**:
  - Quarterly business reviews
  - Custom training sessions
  - Implementation planning
  - Escalation path for critical issues

- Non-critical issues submitted after hours are addressed the next business day

## Response Time SLAs

### Priority Definitions

| Priority | Definition | Examples |
|----------|------------|----------|
| Critical | Service is down or severely degraded for multiple users | Cannot log in, data loss, security breach |
| High | Major feature is broken for a user/team | Cannot create tasks, integrations failing |
| Medium | Feature works but with issues | Slow performance, minor UI bugs |
| Low | Questions, suggestions, minor issues | How-to questions, feature requests |

### Response Times by Plan

| Priority | Starter | Pro | Enterprise |
|----------|---------|-----|------------|
| Critical | 24 hours | 4 hours | 1 hour |
| High | 24 hours | 4 hours | 2 hours |
| Medium | 48 hours | 8 hours | 4 hours |
| Low | 48 hours | 24 hours | 8 hours |

**Note**: Response time is when we first respond to acknowledge the issue. Resolution time depends on complexity.

## Service Level Agreements (SLAs)

### Uptime Guarantees

| Plan | Uptime SLA | Monthly Downtime Allowed |
|------|------------|--------------------------|
| Free | None | N/A |
| Starter | None | N/A |
| Pro | 99.9% | ~43 minutes |
| Enterprise | 99.99% | ~4 minutes |

### SLA Credits
If we fail to meet uptime SLAs:
- **99.9% SLA (Pro)**: 10% credit for each 0.1% below SLA (max 30%)
- **99.99% SLA (Enterprise)**: 10% credit for each 0.01% below SLA (max 50%)

Credits are applied to the next billing cycle upon request. Submit credit requests to billing@techflow.com within 30 days of the incident.

### Exclusions
SLAs do not cover downtime due to:
- Scheduled maintenance (announced 48+ hours in advance)
- Third-party service outages (AWS, payment processors, etc.)
- Customer actions (API abuse, security violations)
- Force majeure events

## Data & Privacy

### Data Retention
- Active accounts: Data retained indefinitely while subscription is active
- Cancelled accounts: Data retained for 30 days, then permanently deleted
- Free accounts inactive for 12+ months: Data may be deleted after notification

### Data Export
Export your data anytime:
- Go to Settings > Account > Export Data
- Choose format: JSON or CSV
- Export includes: Projects, tasks, comments, attachments, time entries
- Processing time: Up to 24 hours for large accounts

### Data Deletion Requests
To permanently delete your data:
1. Cancel your subscription
2. Email privacy@techflow.com requesting deletion
3. We'll confirm deletion within 30 days

### GDPR Compliance
For EU users:
- Right to access: Request a copy of your data
- Right to rectification: Update incorrect data in Settings
- Right to erasure: Request deletion via privacy@techflow.com
- Right to portability: Use data export feature
- Data Processing Agreement available for Enterprise customers

## Incident Communication

### Status Page
Real-time service status: status.techflow.com

Subscribe to updates via:
- Email notifications
- SMS alerts
- RSS feed
- Slack integration

### Incident Response
When an incident occurs:
1. **Identification**: We detect the issue (monitoring or user report)
2. **Investigation**: Engineering team begins diagnosis
3. **Communication**: Status page updated within 15 minutes
4. **Updates**: Regular updates every 30 minutes during active incidents
5. **Resolution**: Issue resolved and confirmed
6. **Post-mortem**: Root cause analysis published within 5 business days for major incidents
