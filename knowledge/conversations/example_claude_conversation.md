# Customer Support AI Agent Design and Planning

## Summary

The user requested help building an AI assistant to answer questions about their company's policies using documentation files. The assistant helped design a customer support agent for TechFlow Solutions that can handle routine inquiries about pricing, features, policies, and troubleshooting.

During the discovery phase, the user selected preferences for agent behavior: friendly but professional tone (1B), apologize and suggest human support for unknown questions (2A), provide comprehensive answers to multi-part questions (3A), and escalate billing disputes, account issues, and frustrated customers to human support (4C).

The architect skill created comprehensive design and implementation documents. The design specifies two main tools: `search_knowledge_base` for finding information in markdown documentation files, and `check_escalation_needed` for detecting situations requiring human intervention. The implementation plan outlines 7 phases from dependency installation through manual testing, with specific test cases for validation.

## Decisions Made

1. **Agent personality: friendly but professional tone** - Balances approachability with business appropriateness for B2B customer support
2. **Apologize and suggest human support for unknown questions** - Maintains trust by acknowledging limitations rather than guessing or hallucinating
3. **Provide comprehensive answers to multi-part questions** - Reduces back-and-forth by addressing all aspects of customer inquiries at once
4. **Broad escalation policy including billing, account, and frustrated customers** - Ensures sensitive situations get human attention while maintaining customer satisfaction
5. **Two-tool approach: knowledge base search and escalation detection** - Separates information retrieval from escalation logic for better maintainability and testing
