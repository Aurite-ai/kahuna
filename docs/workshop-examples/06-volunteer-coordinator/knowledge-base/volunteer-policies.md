# Volunteer Scheduling Policies

## Shift Requirements

### Minimum Coverage

| Role | Morning Shift | Afternoon Shift | Skills Required |
|------|---------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Sorter | 4 volunteers | 3 volunteers | food_handling |
| Packer | 6 volunteers | 4 volunteers | food_handling |
| Driver | 2 volunteers | 2 volunteers | driver (valid license, clean record) |
| Greeter | 2 volunteers | 2 volunteers | None (bilingual preferred) |
| Warehouse | 2 volunteers | 1 volunteer | forklift (if operating equipment) |

### Shift Times

- **Morning Shift:** 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM (4 hours)
- **Afternoon Shift:** 1:00 PM - 5:00 PM (4 hours)
- **Full Day:** Both shifts with 1-hour lunch break (not counted in hours)

## Volunteer Matching Criteria

### Priority Order for Shift Assignment

1. **Skills match** - Volunteer has required skills for the role
2. **Availability match** - Volunteer marked available for that day/time
3. **Reliability score** - Prefer volunteers with 80+ reliability score
4. **Hours balance** - Prefer volunteers with fewer hours this month (equitable distribution)
5. **Role familiarity** - Volunteer has worked this role before

### Automatic Disqualification

Do NOT assign a volunteer if:
- Status is "inactive" or "on_hold"
- Already assigned to another shift on the same day
- Has worked 3+ shifts in the past 7 days (burnout prevention)
- Reliability score below 50 without supervisor approval

## Scheduling Rules

### Advance Notice Requirements

| Action | Minimum Notice |
|--------|----------------|
| New shift assignment | 72 hours before shift |
| Shift cancellation by volunteer | 24 hours before shift |
| Emergency coverage request | Can be same-day |

### Maximum Commitments

- **Weekly maximum:** 4 shifts per volunteer
- **Monthly maximum:** 12 shifts per volunteer
- **Consecutive days:** Maximum 3 days in a row

### Waitlist Protocol

When a shift is full:
1. Add volunteer to waitlist for that shift
2. If cancellation occurs, offer to waitlisted volunteers in order added
3. Waitlisted volunteers have 4 hours to respond before moving to next

## Communication Standards

### Reminder Schedule

| Timing | Message Type | Channel |
|--------|--------------|---------|
| 48 hours before | Shift reminder with details | Email |
| 2 hours before | Day-of reminder | SMS (if opted in) |
| Post-shift | Thank you + hours logged | Email |

### Confirmation Requirements

- All shift assignments require volunteer confirmation within 48 hours
- Unconfirmed assignments after 48 hours: send follow-up, then reassign after 24 more hours
- Confirmation can be: email reply, Notion checkbox, or text response

## Attendance Tracking

### Status Definitions

| Status | Definition | Impact on Reliability Score |
|--------|------------|----------------------------|
| Completed | Arrived and worked full shift | +2 points |
| Partial | Arrived but left early (excused) | No change |
| No-show | Did not arrive, no communication | -10 points |
| Cancelled (24h+) | Cancelled with adequate notice | No change |
| Cancelled (<24h) | Late cancellation | -3 points |

### Reliability Score Calculation

- Starting score for new volunteers: 75
- Maximum score: 100
- Minimum score: 0
- Score below 50 triggers review conversation with volunteer coordinator

## Role-Specific Requirements

### Food Handling (Sorter, Packer)

- Must complete food safety orientation (1-hour online course)
- Certification valid for 2 years
- Cannot handle food with open cuts or illness symptoms

### Driver

- Valid driver's license (verified annually)
- Clean driving record (no DUIs, no at-fault accidents in 3 years)
- Must be 21+ years old
- Familiar with delivery routes (training ride-along required)

### Forklift Operator

- Forklift certification required
- Certification must be current (renewed every 3 years)
- Only certified operators may operate warehouse equipment

### Bilingual Support

- Bilingual volunteers prioritized for Greeter role during distribution
- Spanish and Vietnamese most needed
- Self-reported proficiency accepted

## Emergency Coverage Protocol

### When Coverage Falls Below Minimum

1. **First:** Contact waitlisted volunteers for that shift
2. **Second:** Contact high-reliability volunteers available that day
3. **Third:** Contact escalation list (see `escalation-contacts.md`)
4. **Fourth:** Alert Volunteer Coordinator for staff coverage decision

### Escalation Triggers

Contact Volunteer Coordinator immediately if:
- Shift is <4 hours away and still understaffed
- Driver shift has no coverage (impacts deliveries)
- Multiple volunteers cancel from same shift
- Any safety or conduct concerns

## Special Circumstances

### Holiday Scheduling

- Thanksgiving week: Extended hours, double volunteer needs
- Christmas/New Year: Modified schedule (check annual calendar)
- Summer months: Reduced coverage acceptable (schools out)

### Inclement Weather

- Food bank follows county government closure decisions
- If county offices close, food bank closes
- Volunteers notified via email and SMS within 1 hour of decision
- Cancelled shifts do not affect reliability scores

### New Volunteer Onboarding

- First 3 shifts must be Sorter or Greeter role (lower complexity)
- Paired with experienced volunteer for first shift
- Reliability score tracking begins after third shift
