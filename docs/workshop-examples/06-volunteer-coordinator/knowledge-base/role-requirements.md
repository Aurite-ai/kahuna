# Role Requirements

## Overview

This document defines the skills, certifications, and qualifications required for each volunteer role at Helping Hands Food Bank. Use this to ensure volunteers are matched to appropriate shifts.

---

## Role: Sorter

**Description:** Sort donated food items by category, check expiration dates, and remove damaged goods.

### Requirements

| Requirement | Type | Notes |
|-------------|------|-------|
| Food Handling Certification | Required | Must complete online orientation |
| Physical Ability | Required | Standing for 4 hours, lifting up to 25 lbs |
| Age | Required | 16+ years old |

### Skills (Notion Values)

- `food_handling` - **Required**

### Training

- Online food safety orientation (1 hour) - valid for 2 years
- On-site orientation with shift lead (first shift only)

### Good Fit For

- New volunteers (entry-level role)
- Volunteers who prefer solo work
- Detail-oriented individuals

---

## Role: Packer

**Description:** Pack food boxes according to family size, dietary restrictions, and distribution guidelines.

### Requirements

| Requirement | Type | Notes |
|-------------|------|-------|
| Food Handling Certification | Required | Same as Sorter |
| Physical Ability | Required | Standing, bending, lifting up to 30 lbs |
| Reading Comprehension | Required | Must follow packing lists accurately |
| Age | Required | 16+ years old |

### Skills (Notion Values)

- `food_handling` - **Required**
- `bilingual_spanish` - Preferred (helpful for labeling)
- `bilingual_vietnamese` - Preferred (helpful for labeling)

### Training

- Online food safety orientation (1 hour)
- Packing guidelines walkthrough (30 minutes, first shift)

### Good Fit For

- Volunteers who enjoy systematic work
- Team-oriented individuals
- Those who can work efficiently under time pressure

---

## Role: Driver

**Description:** Deliver food boxes to homebound clients, senior centers, and partner distribution sites.

### Requirements

| Requirement | Type | Notes |
|-------------|------|-------|
| Valid Driver's License | Required | Verified annually |
| Clean Driving Record | Required | No DUIs, no at-fault accidents in 3 years |
| Insurance | Required | Personal auto insurance covering volunteer driving |
| Age | Required | 21+ years old |
| Smartphone | Required | For navigation and delivery confirmation |

### Skills (Notion Values)

- `driver` - **Required**
- `bilingual_spanish` - Preferred (client communication)
- `bilingual_vietnamese` - Preferred (client communication)

### Training

- Route training ride-along (2 hours, before first solo shift)
- Delivery protocol review (30 minutes)
- Client interaction guidelines (online, 30 minutes)

### Vehicle Requirements

If using personal vehicle:
- Trunk/cargo space for minimum 20 boxes
- Functioning AC (for food safety in summer)
- Clean interior for food transport

Food bank vehicles:
- Pre-trip inspection required
- Must complete van certification (additional 1-hour training)

### Good Fit For

- Volunteers who prefer independent work
- Those comfortable with client interaction
- Reliable, punctual individuals

## Role: Greeter

**Description:** Welcome clients, verify appointments, manage check-in, provide information about services.

### Requirements

| Requirement | Type | Notes |
|-------------|------|-------|
| Customer Service Skills | Required | Friendly, patient demeanor |
| Basic Computer Skills | Required | Using check-in tablet |
| Age | Required | 18+ years old |

### Skills (Notion Values)

- No specific skill required
- `bilingual_spanish` - **Highly Preferred** (many clients Spanish-speaking)
- `bilingual_vietnamese` - **Highly Preferred** (growing Vietnamese community)

### Training

- Check-in system walkthrough (30 minutes)
- Client services overview (30 minutes)
- Conflict de-escalation basics (online, 30 minutes)

### Good Fit For

- Extroverted, people-oriented volunteers
- Bilingual individuals
- Those who can remain calm under pressure

---

## Role: Warehouse

**Description:** Receive deliveries, operate equipment, organize storage, maintain inventory.

### Requirements

| Requirement | Type | Notes |
|-------------|------|-------|
| Physical Ability | Required | Heavy lifting (up to 50 lbs), physical work |
| Safety Awareness | Required | Understanding of warehouse safety |
| Age | Required | 18+ years old |

### Skills (Notion Values)

- `forklift` - Required for operating powered equipment
- `food_handling` - Preferred (for organizing food storage)

### Training

- Warehouse safety orientation (1 hour)
- Forklift certification (if operating) - separate 4-hour training
- Inventory system basics (30 minutes)

### Equipment Operation

| Equipment | Certification Required | Training Time |
|-----------|----------------------|---------------|
| Pallet jack (manual) | No | 15-minute briefing |
| Electric pallet jack | Yes | 2-hour training |
| Forklift | Yes | 4-hour certification |

### Good Fit For

- Physically active volunteers
- Those with warehouse/logistics experience
- Detail-oriented individuals for inventory work

## Certification Tracking

### Food Handling Certification

| Status | Meaning | Action |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Valid | Completed within last 2 years | Eligible for food roles |
| Expiring | Within 30 days of expiration | Send renewal reminder |
| Expired | Past expiration date | Cannot assign to food roles |
| Never Certified | New volunteer | Must complete before first food shift |

**Online Course:** [Link to food safety training] - Takes ~1 hour, certificate emailed upon completion.

### Forklift Certification

| Status | Meaning | Action |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Valid | Completed within last 3 years | Eligible for forklift operation |
| Expiring | Within 60 days of expiration | Schedule recertification |
| Expired | Past expiration date | Cannot operate forklift |
| Not Certified | Has not completed | Cannot operate forklift |

**Certification:** Scheduled monthly, Saturday mornings. Contact warehouse manager to register.

### Driver Verification

Annual requirements:
1. Valid license - copy on file
2. Insurance verification - copy on file
3. Driving record check - run each January

## Special Considerations

### Youth Volunteers (16-17)

- Eligible roles: Sorter, Packer only
- Must have parent/guardian waiver on file
- Cannot work past 8pm
- Cannot operate any powered equipment
- Must be paired with adult volunteer

### Court-Ordered Community Service

- Eligible roles: Sorter, Packer, Warehouse (non-equipment)
- Require supervisor sign-off on hours
- Cannot handle cash or client information
- Maximum 4-hour shifts

### Corporate/Group Volunteers

- Pre-assign to Sorter or Packer roles (simplest training)
- Provide group orientation at shift start
- Assign experienced volunteer as group lead
- Maximum 15 per shift (space constraints)
