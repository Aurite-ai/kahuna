## Emergency Procedures

### Complete Shift Cancellation

If a shift must be cancelled due to insufficient coverage:

1. Notify Operations Director for approval
2. Contact all assigned volunteers
3. Post notice on volunteer portal
4. Document reason in shift notes
5. Follow up with affected volunteers within 24 hours

### Volunteer Emergency During Shift

If a volunteer has a medical or safety emergency:

1. Call 911 if medical emergency
2. Notify shift lead immediately
3. Contact Volunteer Coordinator
4. Complete incident report within 24 hours
5. Follow up with volunteer and witnesses

### Weather or Facility Emergency

Food bank closure follows county government:

1. Operations Director makes closure decision by 6am
2. All volunteers notified via email and SMS
3. Post on social media and website
4. Reschedule critical activities (deliveries)
