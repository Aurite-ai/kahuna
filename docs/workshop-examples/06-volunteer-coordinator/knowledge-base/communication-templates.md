# Communication Templates

## Overview

This document contains approved message templates for volunteer communications. Use these templates to ensure consistent, professional communication with all volunteers.

---

## Shift Assignment

### New Shift Assignment

**Subject:** Helping Hands Shift: [Role] on [Day, Date]

```
Hi [First Name]!

You've been assigned to a volunteer shift at Helping Hands Food Bank:

📅 Date: [Day], [Month Date, Year]
⏰ Time: [Start Time] - [End Time]
📍 Location: [Location/Address]
👔 Role: [Role Name]

Please confirm your attendance by [Confirmation Deadline].

What to bring:
- Comfortable, closed-toe shoes
- Water bottle
- [Role-specific items if any]

Questions? Reply to this email or contact Maria at (555) 234-5678.

Thank you for making a difference in our community!

— The Helping Hands Team
```

## Cancellation Handling

### Volunteer Cancellation - Adequate Notice (24+ hours)

**Subject:** RE: Shift Cancellation Confirmed

```
Hi [First Name],

No problem! Your shift on [Date] has been cancelled.

We understand things come up. If you'd like to reschedule,
here are some upcoming shifts that need coverage:

- [Day, Date] [Time] - [Role]
- [Day, Date] [Time] - [Role]
- [Day, Date] [Time] - [Role]

Let us know if any of these work for you!

Thanks for letting us know in advance.

— The Helping Hands Team
```

### Volunteer Cancellation - Late Notice (<24 hours)

**Subject:** RE: Shift Cancellation Confirmed

```
Hi [First Name],

We've received your cancellation for [Date] and understand
that emergencies happen.

As a reminder, our policy asks for 24+ hours notice when
possible, so we have time to find coverage. Late cancellations
can leave our team short-staffed.

If you're having trouble making your scheduled shifts, we're
happy to adjust your availability in our system - just let us know!

We hope everything is okay and look forward to seeing you
at a future shift.

— Maria Santos, Volunteer Coordinator
```

### Cancellation by Food Bank

**Subject:** CANCELLED: Your Shift on [Date]

```
Hi [First Name],

Unfortunately, we need to cancel your volunteer shift on [Date].

Reason: [Weather closure / Facility issue / Other]

We're sorry for any inconvenience! Your reliability score
will not be affected by this cancellation.

[If rescheduling available:]
We're looking for volunteers to cover a make-up shift on
[Alternative Date/Time]. Would you be available?

Thank you for your understanding and commitment to
Helping Hands Food Bank.

— The Helping Hands Team
```

## No-Show Follow-Up

### First No-Show

**Subject:** We Missed You on [Date]

```
Hi [First Name],

We missed you at your scheduled shift on [Date] and wanted
to make sure everything is okay.

If something came up and you weren't able to let us know,
we completely understand - life happens!

For future shifts, please try to notify us if you can't
make it so we can find coverage. You can:
- Reply to any reminder email
- Text or call (555) 234-5678
- Cancel in the volunteer portal

We hope to see you at a future shift!

Best,
— Maria Santos, Volunteer Coordinator
```

### Second No-Show

**Subject:** Checking In: Missed Shifts

```
Hi [First Name],

We've noticed you've missed your last two scheduled shifts
without notification. We wanted to reach out to see if
everything is alright.

If your schedule or circumstances have changed, we'd be
happy to:
- Update your availability in our system
- Switch you to different shift times
- Discuss any challenges you're facing

No judgment - we just want to make sure volunteering works
for your life! And if you need to take a break, that's
completely okay too.

Please give me a call when you have a moment: (555) 234-5678.

Take care,
— Maria Santos, Volunteer Coordinator
```

### Third No-Show (Status Change)

**Subject:** Your Volunteer Status

```
Hi [First Name],

After three missed shifts without notification, we've
updated your volunteer status to "on hold" in our system.

This isn't a punishment - we just want to make sure we
can reliably count on scheduled volunteers so we don't
leave shifts understaffed.

If you'd like to return to active status, please:
1. Contact me to discuss your availability
2. Complete one observed shift to reactivate

We value every volunteer and hope to see you back when
the time is right for you.

Best wishes,
— Maria Santos, Volunteer Coordinator
(555) 234-5678 | maria@helpinghands.org
```

## Special Situations

### Inclement Weather

**Subject:** ⚠️ Weather Update: [Date] Shifts

```
Hi [First Name],

Due to [weather condition], Helping Hands Food Bank will
[remain open with caution / close / delay opening].

[If open:]
Your shift is still scheduled. However, your safety comes
first - if conditions in your area make travel unsafe,
please stay home. No penalty will be applied.

[If closed:]
Your shift on [Date] has been cancelled. We'll reach out
about rescheduling options.

[If delayed:]
Morning shifts will begin at [New Time]. Please adjust
your arrival accordingly.

Stay safe!
— The Helping Hands Team
```

## Template Variables

When generating messages, replace these variables:

| Variable | Description | Example |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| `[First Name]` | Volunteer's first name | Sarah |
| `[Date]` | Full date | March 15, 2026 |
| `[Day]` | Day of week | Saturday |
| `[Start Time]` | Shift start | 8:00 AM |
| `[End Time]` | Shift end | 12:00 PM |
| `[Role Name]` | Volunteer role | Packer |
| `[Location]` | Shift location | Main Warehouse |
| `[Shift Lead Name]` | Lead volunteer name | David Kim |
| `[Confirmation Deadline]` | Date to confirm by | Thursday, March 13 |
| `[X]` | Numeric placeholder | Varies |
