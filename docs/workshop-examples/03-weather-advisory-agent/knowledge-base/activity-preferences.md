# Activity Preferences

Personal weather preferences and activity thresholds for the Weather Advisory Agent.

---

## My Activities

### Running

My primary outdoor activity. I run 3-4 times per week, usually in the morning or late afternoon.

| Condition | Ideal Range | Acceptable Range | Hard No |
|-----------|-------------|------------------|---------|
| Temperature | 55-70°F | 40-80°F | Below 25°F or above 90°F |
| Wind | Under 10mph | Under 20mph | Above 25mph |
| Precipitation | 0% | Under 20% | Above 40% or active rain |
| Humidity | 30-60% | 20-80% | Above 90% |

**Notes:**
- I prefer morning runs in summer (before heat builds)
- Evening runs are fine in spring/fall
- Light drizzle is okay; steady rain is not
- I have good cold-weather gear, so cold is more acceptable than heat

### Cycling

Weekend rides, usually 20-40 miles. Weather matters more due to longer exposure.

| Condition | Ideal Range | Acceptable Range | Hard No |
|-----------|-------------|------------------|---------|
| Temperature | 60-75°F | 45-85°F | Below 35°F or above 95°F |
| Wind | Under 12mph | Under 18mph | Above 22mph (dangerous) |
| Precipitation | 0% | Under 10% | Any rain chance for long rides |
| UV Index | Under 6 | Under 8 | Above 10 without sunscreen |

**Notes:**
- Wind direction matters - headwind on the way out means tailwind home
- Road conditions after rain can be dangerous (oil, debris)
- I need at least a 4-hour dry window for longer rides

### Hiking

Weekend activity, usually half-day trips in nearby mountains.

| Condition | Ideal Range | Acceptable Range | Hard No |
|-----------|-------------|------------------|---------|
| Temperature | 55-72°F | 40-80°F | Below 30°F or above 88°F |
| Wind | Under 15mph | Under 25mph | Above 35mph at elevation |
| Precipitation | Under 10% | Under 25% | Above 40% or thunderstorms |
| UV Index | Under 7 | Under 9 | Above 10 requires extra protection |

**Notes:**
- Afternoon thunderstorms are common in mountains - start early!
- Check conditions at trailhead elevation AND summit
- Snow can persist at higher elevations into June

### Gardening

Yard work and garden maintenance. Flexible on conditions.

| Condition | Ideal Range | Acceptable Range | Hard No |
|-----------|-------------|------------------|---------|
| Temperature | 60-78°F | 45-85°F | Below 40°F or above 92°F |
| Wind | Under 15mph | Under 25mph | Above 30mph |
| Precipitation | 0% during work | Light rain okay | Heavy rain or storms |

**Notes:**
- Some tasks are fine in light rain (weeding, harvesting)
- Mowing needs dry grass - wait 24hrs after significant rain
- Overcast days are actually ideal (less sun exposure)

## Commute Information

### Morning Commute
- **Time:** 7:30 AM departure
- **Mode:** Bicycle (April-October), Car (November-March)
- **Duration:** 25 minutes by bike, 15 minutes by car
- **Concerns:** Rain, ice, extreme cold

### Evening Commute
- **Time:** 5:30 PM departure
- **Mode:** Same as morning
- **Duration:** Same as morning
- **Concerns:** Afternoon thunderstorms (summer), darkness (winter)

### Bike Commute Thresholds
- **Will bike if:** Temp 40-90°F, precipitation under 20%, no ice
- **Won't bike if:** Active rain, ice on roads, wind above 25mph
- **Need warning:** If afternoon rain likely, bring rain gear or take car

## Seasonal Adjustments

### Summer (June-August)
- Prefer early morning activities (before 10am)
- Afternoon thunderstorm season - mountain activities should end by 1pm
- Higher UV protection needed
- Hydration reminders welcome

### Fall (September-November)
- Best hiking season - less crowded, cool temps
- Watch for early snow at elevation
- Shorter days - sunset awareness
- Temperature swings can be dramatic (30°F morning to 70°F afternoon)

### Winter (December-February)
- Outdoor activities limited to clear days
- Ice and snow on trails - check conditions
- Bike commuting paused unless roads are clear
- Appreciate any day above 45°F!

### Spring (March-May)
- Wildly variable - could be 70°F or snowing
- Wind season (March especially)
- Mud on trails after snowmelt
- Gradually resume bike commuting
