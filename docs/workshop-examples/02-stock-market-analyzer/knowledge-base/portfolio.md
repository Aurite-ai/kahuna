## Investment Rules

### What I Will Do
- Add to positions on significant dips (10%+ from recent highs)
- Rebalance annually if allocations drift >5% from targets
- Review thesis for each holding quarterly
- Sell if fundamental thesis is broken (not just price drops)

### What I Won't Do
- Panic sell on daily volatility
- Chase meme stocks or "hot tips"
- Use margin or leverage
- Trade options (yet)
