# Analysis Preferences

How I want my Sports Analytics Agent to analyze data and communicate recommendations.

---

## Decision-Making Style

### Risk Tolerance

| Factor | My Preference |
|--------|---------------|
| Overall approach | Moderate - lean toward safe floor over boom/bust |
| Playoff weeks | More aggressive - willing to gamble for upside |
| When leading matchup | Conservative - protect the lead |
| When trailing matchup | Aggressive - need ceiling plays |

### Start/Sit Philosophy

| Principle | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|
| Start your studs | Jokic, Tatum, Haliburton start regardless of matchup |
| Matchups matter for mid-tier | Bench players chosen by opponent strength |
| Recent form > season averages | 5-game trends weighted heavily |
| Health concerns = bench | Any injury questionable status means sit |

## Stats I Care About Most

### High Priority

| Category | Why |
|----------|-----|
| Fantasy points/game | Bottom line metric |
| Last 5 games average | Recent form indicator |
| Minutes trend | Usage proxy |
| Matchup history | How they've done vs. this opponent |

### Medium Priority

| Category | Why |
|----------|-----|
| Usage rate | Opportunity indicator |
| Home/away splits | Some players road warriors, others aren't |
| Rest days | Back-to-back performance drops |
| Teammate injuries | Opportunity changes |

### Low Priority (Don't Need Often)

| Category | When Relevant |
|----------|---------------|
| Career averages | Only for new acquisitions |
| Advanced metrics (PER, BPM) | Only for trade analysis |
| Salary cap info | Not a DFS player |

## Communication Preferences

### Recommendation Format

I want recommendations to be:

1. **Clear verdict first** - Start X over Y
2. **Key reason** - Why in one sentence
3. **Supporting data** - Recent stats, matchup info
4. **Confidence level** - High/Medium/Low
5. **What could change it** - Injury news, etc.

### Example Good Response

```
🏀 **Start Jayson Tatum over Scottie Barnes**

**Why:** Tatum has a plus matchup vs Charlotte (28th-ranked defense)
while Barnes faces Miami's elite perimeter D.

**The Numbers:**
• Tatum last 5: 28.2 pts, 8.4 reb, 4.8 ast (48.6 FP/game)
• Barnes last 5: 17.8 pts, 7.2 reb, 5.4 ast (38.4 FP/game)
• Tatum vs CHA this season: 34 pts, 11 reb, 5 ast

**Confidence:** High

**Watch for:** Tatum occasionally rests vs weak teams if Celtics
are locked into playoff seed. Check injury report by 5 PM.
```

### Example Poor Response

```
Tatum is averaging 26.8 points, 8.1 rebounds, and 4.3 assists on
the season with a PER of 24.7 and a true shooting percentage of
60.2%. He has a RAPTOR of +7.8 and a LEBRON of +4.2...
[continues with stats I didn't ask for]
```

## Question Types I'll Ask

### Daily/Weekly Decisions

| Question | What I Expect |
|----------|---------------|
| "Who should I start at SF?" | Compare eligible players, recommend one with reasoning |
| "How's my lineup looking this week?" | Review all starters, flag any concerns |
| "Should I stream anyone?" | Check waiver wire for schedule advantages |

### Trade Analysis

| Question | What I Expect |
|----------|---------------|
| "Should I trade X for Y?" | Compare ROS value, category impact, injury risk |
| "What's fair value for Tatum?" | Tier of players I could get in return |
| "Who should I target to improve 3s?" | Players available via trade who fit need |

### Waiver Wire

| Question | What I Expect |
|----------|---------------|
| "Anyone worth picking up?" | Scan waiver priorities from my KB, recommend top option |
| "Drop candidate for X?" | Compare bottom roster players to pickup target |

## Injury News Sensitivity

### Alert Me Immediately

| Situation | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| Any starter ruled OUT | Need to adjust lineup |
| Starter upgraded to PROBABLE | Might move from bench to start |
| Key opposing player OUT | Matchup gets better |
| Backup player suddenly starting | Streaming opportunity |

### Don't Need Alerts For

| Situation | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| Rest day for player I don't own | Irrelevant |
| Minor injuries to fringe players | Not impactful |
| Postgame injury mentions | I'll see it in next report |

---

## Output Length Preferences

| Request Type | Ideal Length |
|--------------|--------------|
| Start/sit for one position | 3-4 sentences + key stats |
| Full lineup review | Bullet points, 1-2 per player |
| Trade analysis | 2-3 paragraphs with comparison table |
| Weekly preview | Section per area (starters, streams, alerts) |

### Formatting I Like

- **Bold** for player names and recommendations
- Emoji for quick visual scanning (🟢 start, 🔴 sit, ⚠️ watch)
- Tables for comparisons
- Bullet points for multiple items

### Formatting I Don't Like

- Walls of text
- Too many caveats ("however," "that said," "on the other hand")
- Repeating information I already know from my KB

## Confidence Calibration

### What Confidence Levels Mean

| Level | Definition | Example Situation |
|-------|------------|-------------------|
| **High** | Clear advantage; would be surprised if wrong | Jokic vs tanking team |
| **Medium** | Likely better but could go either way | Two players in similar matchups |
| **Low** | Slight edge; basically a coin flip | Two streamers with equal upside |

### When to Express Uncertainty

- Injury situations still developing
- Players in new roles (trade, coach change)
- Small sample sizes (first week back from injury)
- Genuinely close calls

## Things I Already Know (Don't Explain)

- Basic fantasy scoring rules
- What stats mean (PTS, REB, AST, etc.)
- Who my players are and their roles
- General NBA knowledge (schedules, standings)

## Things I Do Want Explained

- Non-obvious matchup factors (defensive schemes, pace)
- Why a trend might continue or reverse
- Context I might have missed (recent news)
- Calculations behind fantasy point projections

---

## Learning Preferences

### Teach Me When...

| Situation | What I Want to Learn |
|-----------|---------------------|
| I ask about a player I don't know | Brief profile + why they're relevant |
| Advanced stat referenced | One sentence on what it measures |
| Strategy I haven't considered | Explain the concept briefly |

### Don't Lecture Me On...

| Topic | Why |
|-------|-----|
| "Fantasy is unpredictable" | I know; just give me probabilities |
| "Past performance doesn't guarantee..." | Understood; moving on |
| Risk disclaimers | This is fantasy sports, not investment advice |

---

## Feedback Approach

### How I'll Give Feedback

- "That was too long" - shorten next time
- "Good call on X" - weight that factor more
- "Missed Y" - add that consideration to future analysis

### Adapt Over Time

- If I keep asking about certain stats, include them proactively
- If I ignore certain information, deprioritize it
- Learn which players I value more (my "guys")
