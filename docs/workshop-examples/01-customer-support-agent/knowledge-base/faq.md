# TechFlow Pro - Frequently Asked Questions

## Getting Started

### How do I create a new project?
Click the "New Project" button in the top-right corner of your dashboard. Enter a project name, select a template (or start blank), and invite team members. Your project will be ready in seconds.

### How do I invite team members?
Go to Project Settings > Team Members > Invite. Enter their email addresses and select their role (Admin, Editor, or Viewer). They'll receive an email invitation to join.

### What's the difference between Workspaces and Projects?
A **Workspace** is your organization's top-level container that holds all your projects. A **Project** is an individual initiative with its own tasks, timeline, and team. You can have multiple projects within one workspace.

## Account & Billing

### How do I upgrade my plan?
Go to Settings > Billing > Change Plan. Select your new plan and enter payment details. The upgrade takes effect immediately, and you'll be charged a prorated amount.

### Can I switch from monthly to annual billing?
Yes! Annual billing saves you 20%. Go to Settings > Billing > Switch to Annual. Your new rate applies at the next billing cycle.

### How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period. You can export your data anytime before cancellation.

### Do you offer refunds?
We offer a full refund within 14 days of your initial purchase. After that, we don't provide refunds, but you can cancel anytime to stop future charges. Contact support@techflow.com for refund requests.

## Features

### Can I integrate with other tools?
Yes! TechFlow Pro integrates with:
- Slack (notifications and updates)
- Google Calendar (deadline syncing)
- GitHub (link commits to tasks)
- Jira (import/export projects)
- Zapier (connect to 3000+ apps)

### Does TechFlow work offline?
The desktop app supports offline mode. Changes sync automatically when you reconnect. The web app requires an internet connection.

### How do I set up automated workflows?
Go to Project Settings > Automations > Create Rule. Choose a trigger (e.g., "Task moved to Done"), set conditions, and define actions (e.g., "Send Slack message"). Pro and Enterprise plans include unlimited automations.

### Can I customize task fields?
Yes, on Pro and Enterprise plans. Go to Project Settings > Custom Fields to add text, number, date, dropdown, or checkbox fields to your tasks.

## Security

### Is my data secure?
Yes. TechFlow uses:
- AES-256 encryption at rest
- TLS 1.3 encryption in transit
- SOC 2 Type II compliance
- GDPR compliance
- Daily backups with 30-day retention

### Do you offer Single Sign-On (SSO)?
SSO is available on Enterprise plans. We support SAML 2.0 with providers like Okta, Azure AD, and OneLogin.

### Can I enable two-factor authentication?
Yes! Go to Account Settings > Security > Enable 2FA. We support authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy) and SMS verification.

## Troubleshooting

### I forgot my password
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email, and we'll send a reset link valid for 24 hours.

### I'm not receiving email notifications
Check your spam folder first. Then go to Settings > Notifications to verify your preferences. If issues persist, add notifications@techflow.com to your email whitelist.

### The app is running slowly
Try these steps:
1. Clear your browser cache
2. Disable browser extensions
3. Check your internet connection
4. Try a different browser
If problems continue, contact support with your browser version and a screenshot.
